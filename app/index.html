What is DrupalCamp TLV?

It's a one day Drupal event with training and Advanced Drupal sessions.

Why should I come?

Breaking your daily routine

This day is a special day. You won’t go to your office and look on your dull emails. Your’e going to participate in an awesome events with awesome people.
