About the camp


What is DrupalCamp?
The annual Tel Aviv conference for the Drupal Community to learn and schmooze.
Join us this November 29th, 2016 for a jam-packed day of interesting summits and trainings.
Attend the keynote by Bojan the Great. Socialize with great people over food and plenty of beer and wine.

New to Drupal?
This is a great opportunity to discover this amazing system and its vibrant community.

Experienced with Drupal?
Come and see what the future holds, because Drupal never stops!
